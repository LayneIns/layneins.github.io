About Me

Hi! My name is Hui Liu.
I am currently a first-year PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Queen's University. My advisor is Prof. Xiaodan Zhu.
Before coming to Queen's, I received my bachelor's degree from the School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science at Peking University in 2018.

My main research interests include natural language processing, text mining and machine learning.
